Worlds & Codices

A selection of my primary universes. Each world has its own codex: lore, magic systems, character rosters, and long-form arcs designed for novels, campaigns, or future adaptations.

Fae Land Mythos

Mythic Fantasy

A layered faerie cosmology spanning god eras, primordial predators, courtly intrigues, and the tragedies that shaped mortal history.

  • Era timelines from God Era to Primordial and beyond
  • Species codices for Pure Faeries, elder beings, and more
  • Relics, travel tools, and cultural structures

Jay Stellazio & the Three Suitors

Family Saga

A sprawling, character-driven tale of a gentle summoner at the center of a powerful family and three dangerous suitors.

  • Stellazio family tree and sibling codex
  • Double Star Element magic and combat roles
  • Seasonal episode outlines and romantic tension arcs

Celestine & the Runaway Angels

Celestial Drama

A world of angelic classifications, runaway wards, Messiahs, and divine politics set against a backdrop of trauma and found family.

  • Angel codex: choirs, classes, and runaway types
  • Relics, lightning diviners, and heavenly factions
  • Episode structures blending slice-of-life and spiritual conflict

Falflur Peri Tribes of Xanadu

Tribal Fantasy

A valley of floral Peri cultures, sacred fauna, ambrosias, and secret cults balancing joy, decay, and duty.

  • Yellow Falflur Peri settlements and guild professions
  • Flora, fauna, and ambrosia crafting traditions
  • Cults, Sunblinded Children, and hidden inquisitors

Corbin’s Confectionary

Cozy Fantasy

A soft, sugar-dusted world centered around a magical confection shop, found family, and quiet acts of care.

  • Confectionary layout, staff, and local cast
  • Magical sweets, candy creatures, and rituals of comfort
  • Episode outlines blending romance and everyday magic

Universal Magic Framework

Core System

A shared spell and magic structure that quietly threads through multiple worlds while allowing each setting its own flavor.

  • Elemental and thematic classifications of spells
  • Guidelines for how different worlds express the same roots
  • Support for long-form arcs, stakes, and character growth
