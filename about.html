About the Creator

I write in long arcs, big families, and quiet, intense emotions. JunoLibrary is my way of giving those worlds a place to live together.

I’m Juno – a fantasy worldbuilder who designs deep codices, ensemble casts, and interconnected universes. I like stories where people are messy, magic has rules, and love shows up in strange, stubborn ways.

My work leans into themes of found family, trauma and recovery, devotion, and the complicated ways people save each other without always knowing they’re doing it.

What I Tend to Build

  • Story codices and world bibles with timelines, magic, and social structures
  • Large, emotionally tangled families and recurring cast lineups
  • Magic systems that can be reused quietly across multiple worlds
  • Species, flora, fauna, and ecologies that feel lived-in and specific
  • Episode-style arcs suited for serial fiction or campaign play
