The Bestiary Wing

Alien anatomies, soft horrors, and quiet monsters.

This wing of the library catalogs off-world species, battle-bred morphs, and strange evolutions from across the multiverse. Everything here is meant as a concept reference, not a final design.

Disclaimer: All character and anatomy images in this Bestiary were created using generative AI and may be redrawn or replaced by hand-crafted artwork in future revisions.

1. Mavins

Planet of Origin: Unnamed dream-moon
Elemental Affinity: Moon, Psychic, Aurora
Magical Prowess: ★★★★☆
Technology Level: Soul- and dream-based constructs, bio-crystalline architecture

Physical Appearance

  • Slender humanoids with opalescent, softly glowing skin.
  • Long hair that drifts like nebula dust.
  • Lower body resembles a smooth, glistening slug-tail.
  • Large luminous “dream eyes”.
  • During metamorphosis they manifest temporary wings of light / aurora plumes.

Core Traits

  • Larva → chrysalis → adult dream-shaper life cycle.
  • Walk dreams, harvest emotional energy, and condense it into Dream Pearls.
  • Strong telepathy and empathy; weave auroral light and mana currents.
  • Prefer psychic defense and emotional harmonics over direct violence.

2. Yasin

Planet of Origin: Haven-Serra
Elemental Affinity: Light & Shadow (by sect)
Magical Prowess: ★★☆☆☆–★★★★☆ (varies)
Technology Level: Tribal mystic-tech, divine relic forging

Shared Anatomy

  • Serpentine lower body with a long scaled tail.
  • Upright torso with humanoid arms and clawed hands.
  • Cobra-like hood framed by feather-spines.
  • Narrow, predatory face; slit pupils; venomous fangs.

2.1 White Cobra Sect

Role: Healers, historians, spiritual mediators.

  • White, silver, and pale-gold feathers and scales.
  • Soft luminous hood markings and ceremonial jewelry.
  • Healing venom; purifying breath and sigil-magic.
  • Trance-songs that soothe beasts and cleanse curses.

2.2 Black Asp Sect

Role: Executioners, elite soldiers, shadow-hunters.

  • Dark scales, black hoods, green or violet feather-spines.
  • Lean, aggressive build with segmented armor.
  • Shadow-infused venom and stealth tactics.
  • Strict ranking system defined by hood crests and feathers.

3. Crole

Planet of Origin: Gulik (frozen world)
Elemental Affinity: Ice
Magical Prowess: ★☆☆☆☆
Technology Level: Stone-age tools amplified by rare crystal weapons

The Crole are a half-human, half-insect people who survived near extinction in endless blizzards. Their population rebuilt itself inside underground sanctuaries before expanding into multiple fiercely competing tribes.

Dual Forms

  • Insect Form – fully cold-immune, powerful and agile, but sterile. Used for hunting, war, and surface survival.
  • Humanoid Form – human-like with residual insect traits, vulnerable to the cold but fertile. Revealing this form is an act of extreme trust.

3.1 Male Crole

  • Mantis-inspired biped with raptorial forearms or scythe-like blades.
  • Digitigrade legs with clawed feet and hard chitin plating.
  • Tribal paint from fallen beasts marking tribe, rank, and achievements.

3.2 Female Crole

  • Thick insulating fur and large patterned wings.
  • Slender torso with long legs and taloned feet.
  • Soft chitin at joints, dense fluff around neck and hips.
  • Variants: weavers, brood-mother caretakers, monstrous warriors, and standard slender hunters.

4. Midomi

Planet of Origin: Sofado
Elemental Affinity: Lunar & Devil
Magical Prowess: ★★☆☆☆
Technology Level: Human-equivalent, adapted to multi-limb ergonomics

The Midomi are an all-male humanoid species with white skin, brown hair, and seven prehensile tentacles emerging from the back. Their society recognizes two main base morphs, plus a number of rare high-breed variants.

4.1 Pink Morph — Base Form

  • Height around 6'3", slender and boyish in build.
  • Bright pink tentacles with high flexibility and sensitivity.
  • Often occupy artistic, spiritual, and interpersonal roles.
  • Primary impregnators for Green Morph egg carriers.

4.2 Green Morph — Base Form

  • Height 7'5"–8'0", heavily muscular.
  • Vivid green tentacles suited to combat and heavy labor.
  • Serve as warriors, hunters, tacticians, and leadership candidates.
  • Often perceived as intimidating but possess high intelligence.

4.3 Pink High-Breed Variants

Additional angles: midomi-pink-highbreeds.png (5–6).jpeg.

  • Matriarch – vast tentacular entity that forms the Dream Network, a psychic hive-mind of nearby eggs.
  • Pastor – alien-faced diplomats who preserve planetary peace and handle interstellar negotiations.
  • Custodian – silent enforcers with invisible bodies and devastating Brain Shock psychic blasts.
  • Archangel – white-bodied, red-eyed bodyguards dedicated to nurseries and medical facilities.
  • Ethereal Breed – infertile, human-passing Midomi with ghostly telepathic tentacles used for espionage.
  • Nursemaid – elongated psychic specialists who act as the unseen security detail for the Prime Minister.

4.4 Green High-Breed Variants

Additional angles: midomi-green-highbreeds.png (5–8).jpeg.

All Green High Breeds are tied to the Midomi military and defense structure. They emphasize brutal physical force supported by sharp tactical minds.

  • Tormentor – poison-laden interrogators with hallucinogenic right tentacles and neurotoxic left tentacles.
  • Champion – apex soldiers with perfect night vision; allowed four wives and dedicated nurseries.
  • Tactician – human-looking base form, monstrous ascended form; brilliant strategists with minor telekinesis.
  • Warlord – battlefield titans whose tentacles can shred war machines; limited by fragile hearts.
  • Olympian – web-footed honor guard and planetary police, protecting all sites outside medical grounds.
  • Titan – feral rage-forms that imprint on an infertile Pink handler and must be kept calm at all costs.
  • Atlas – hyper-intelligent leaders with tentacles that can block artillery; often serve as Prime Ministers.
  • “Hunk” Variant – genetically normal Green Morphs who are simply, spectacularly attractive to Pink Morphs.
