The Bestiary Wing

Strange anatomies from quiet corners of the multiverse.

This gallery catalogs alien species, morphs, and high-breed variants that drift through JunoLibrary’s shared universes. Think of it as a scientific field report written by an over-caffeinated stargazer.

Generative AI Disclaimer: All character and anatomy images in this Bestiary were created with generative AI and are used as conceptual references only. Final designs may be redrawn or revised by hand in the future.

1. Mavins

Planet of Origin: Unnamed dream-moon

Elemental Affinity: Moon, Psychic, Aurora

Magical Prowess: ★★★★☆

Technology Level: Soul- and dream-based constructs, bio-crystalline architecture

1.1 Physical Appearance

  • Slender humanoids with opalescent, softly glowing skin.
  • Long hair that drifts like nebula dust.
  • Lower body resembling a smooth, glistening slug-tail.
  • Large luminous “dream eyes.”
  • During metamorphosis, they manifest temporary wings of light or aurora plumes.

1.2 Core Traits

  • Life cycle runs from larva → chrysalis → adult dream-shaper.
  • Can walk dreams, harvest emotional energy, and condense it into Dream Pearls.
  • Strong telepathy and empathy; they weave auroral light and mana currents.
  • Avoid direct violence and specialize in psychic defense and emotional harmonics.

2. Yasin

Planet of Origin: Haven-Serra

Elemental Affinity: Light & Shadow (by sect)

Magical Prowess: ★★☆☆☆–★★★★☆ (varies)

Technology Level: Tribal mystic-tech, divine relic forging

2.1 Shared Anatomy

  • Serpentine lower body with a long scaled tail.
  • Upright torso with humanoid arms and clawed hands.
  • Cobra-like hood framed by feather-spines.
  • Narrow, predatory face with slit pupils and venomous fangs.

2.2 White Cobra Sect

Role: Healers, historians, and spiritual mediators.

Visual Cues

  • White, silver, and pale-gold feathers and scales.
  • Soft luminous markings along the hood.
  • Calm posture, open hands, ceremonial jewelry and robes.

Key Traits

  • Healing venom and purifying breath paired with sigil-magic.
  • Trance-songs that soothe beasts and cleanse curses.
  • Operate shrines and sanctuaries, serving as judges and diplomats.

2.3 Black Asp Sect

Role: Executioners, elite soldiers, and shadow-hunters.

Visual Cues

  • Dark scales and black hoods with green or violet feather-spines.
  • Lean, aggressive builds with segmented armor and blade-like feathers.
  • Feathered crest doubles as a visible rank marker (longer = higher rank).

Key Traits

  • Shadow-infused venom and stealth tactics.
  • Specialists in hunting curses, monsters, and traitors.
  • Stratified ranking system, in direct contrast to the egalitarian White Cobra Sect.

3. Crole

Planet of Origin: Gulik (frozen world)

Elemental Affinity: Ice

Magical Prowess: ★☆☆☆☆

Technology Level: Stone-age tools amplified by rare crystal weapons.

3.1 Species Overview

The Crole are a half-human, half-insect people who survived near extinction in endless blizzards. Their population rebuilt itself inside underground sanctuaries sheltered from the worst ice storms, eventually expanding into multiple fiercely competing tribes.

3.2 Dual Forms

  • Insect Form – fully cold-immune, powerful and agile, but sterile. Used for hunting, war, and most surface-level survival.
  • Humanoid Form – human-like with residual insect traits, vulnerable to the cold yet fertile. Revealing this form is an act of extreme trust and vulnerability.

Many Crole remain in insect form permanently and never learn what their true humanoid face looks like.

3.3 Male Crole

  • Refined from mantis-centaur to a bipedal mantis-humanoid silhouette.
  • Digitigrade legs ending in clawed feet.
  • Large raptorial forearms or scythe-like blades for hunting and combat.
  • Hard chitin plating across chest, shoulders, and thighs.
  • Tribal paint made from fallen beasts to mark tribe, rank, and achievements.

3.4 Female Crole

  • Based on the poodle moth: thick insulating fur and large patterned wings.
  • Slender torso, long legs, and taloned feet.
  • Soft chitin at joints with dense fluff around neck and hips.

Common variants include:

  • Weavers – shave portions of fur to insulate nurseries and tunnels.
  • Brood Mothers / Caretakers – rounder, cuter forms signaling high fertility.
  • Monstrous Warriors – bulkier, more bestial silhouettes for elite defense.
  • Standard Slender Form – the most common everyday morphology.

4. Midomi

Planet of Origin: Sofado

Elemental Affinity: Lunar & Devil

Magical Prowess: ★★☆☆☆

Technology Level: Roughly human-equivalent, adapted to multi-limb ergonomics.

4.1 Core Species Traits

  • All-male humanoid species.
  • White skin, brown hair, and seven prehensile tentacles emerging from the back.
  • Two base color morphs:
    • Pink Morph – slim, boyish build with bright pink tentacles.
    • Green Morph – tall, muscular build with vivid green tentacles.
  • Reproduction uses one specialized tentacle as a gamete-producing organ (kept non-explicit and non-anatomical in design).

4.2 Pink Morph — Base Form

Height: ~6'3"

Build: Slender, adolescent-boy aesthetic.

Role: Emotional and cultural heart of Midomi society.

  • Often the primary impregnators of Green Morph egg carriers.
  • Frequently involved in arts, spirituality, and interpersonal networks.

4.3 Green Morph — Base Form

Height: 7'5"–8'0"

Build: Heavily muscular.

Role: Warriors, hunters, tacticians, and leadership candidates.

  • Often perceived as intimidating due to size and darker emotional presentation.
  • Possess high intelligence and strong battlefield instincts.

5. Pink Midomi High-Breed Variants

Pink High Breeds lean into the psychic, spiritual, and diplomatic side of Midomi evolution. Many serve as living infrastructure for Sofado’s religion, security, and government.

5.1 Matriarch

  • Some Pink Morphs transform into a gigantic tentacular entity with a central core mind.
  • Creates a psychic hive-mind of surrounding eggs known as the Dream Network.
  • Eggs placed within its radius share emotions, memories, and dreams before hatching.
  • Worshipped by a devoted faction as a living god.

5.2 Pastor

  • High Breed with an alien-like face and trailing tentacles from head and back.
  • Serves as planetary peacekeeper and diplomat.
  • Specializes in telepathic negotiation and conflict de-escalation.

5.3 Custodian

  • Appears as a large pink tentacle supporting a featureless human head.
  • Silent enforcers and covert security agents.
  • Can become invisible and unleash Brain Shock, a focused psychic blast: one blast disorients; three successive blasts collapse the mind entirely.

5.4 Archangel

  • All-white body with a black mask-like face and red eyes.
  • Elite bodyguards dedicated to nurseries, hospitals, and reproductive facilities.
  • Combine physical strength with precise psychic shielding and rarely leave their wards.

5.5 Ethereal Breed

  • Can pass for fully human at a glance.
  • Born infertile, but bear a cosmic birthmark that manifests ghostly ethereal tentacles.
  • Specialists in long-range telepathy, information gathering, and espionage.

5.6 Nursemaid

  • Elongated head, body composed almost entirely of tentacles, classic alien eyes.
  • Rare High Breeds with extremely strong psychic powers.
  • Assigned to protect and attend the Midomi Prime Minister as an unseen shadow security team.

6. Green Midomi High-Breed Variants

All Green High Breeds are tied to Midomi military or planetary defense. Where Pink High Breeds lean into psychic nuance, Green High Breeds survive on raw force backed by calculated intelligence.

6.1 Tormentor

  • All-green head with a white body and tentacles packed with toxins.
  • Right-side tentacles deliver a lethal hallucinogenic poison.
  • Left-side tentacles inflict a neurotoxin that paralyzes the legs and kills over two weeks.
  • Used in private executions and high-value interrogations.

6.2 Champion

  • Smooth shell-like helmet with two small antennae.
  • Idealized warrior physique; apex soldiers and hunters.
  • Exceptional night vision and tracking skills.
  • Permitted four Midomi wives, with specialized nurseries focused on breeding more Champions.

6.3 Tactician

  • Base form resembles a muscular human with minimal tentacles visible.
  • Ascended form reveals heavy tentacles, green skin, and a monstrous visage.
  • Brilliant strategists and military advisors.
  • Limited psychic ability: weak telekinesis for battlefield control.

6.4 Warlord

  • Massive tentacle bundles projecting from back and shoulders.
  • Capable of lifting and tearing apart war machines and heavy weaponry.
  • Born with fragile hearts that may rupture under extreme adrenaline.
  • Deployed only when a battle absolutely must be turned.

6.5 Olympian

  • White-bodied High Breeds with large webbed, almost aquatic feet.
  • Serve as honor guard for the Midomi homeworld and as elite planetary police.
  • While Archangels guard medical and reproductive sites, Olympians protect everything else.

6.6 Titan

  • Huge, bestial High Breeds driven by raw rage rather than intellect.
  • Each Titan imprints on an infertile Pink Morph handler at hatching.
  • Utterly loyal to their handler but dangerously unstable to everyone else.
  • Society works hard to keep Titans calm, enriched, and far from triggers.

6.7 Atlas

  • White body with massively reinforced tentacles capable of blocking gunfire and artillery.
  • Mask-like tentacle structures around the eyes for additional protection.
  • Hyper-intelligent and often serve as Prime Ministers or top-level leaders.
  • Embodies the Midomi ideal of leadership: power, restraint, and strategy.

6.8 “Hunk” Variant

  • Genetically ordinary Green Morphs with especially striking, attractive features.
  • Physically identical in ability to standard Green Morphs.
  • Considered exotic and highly desirable partners by Pink Morphs.
  • Socially treated as rare treasures despite their lack of extra powers.
