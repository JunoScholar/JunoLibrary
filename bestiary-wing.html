1. Mavins

Planet of Origin: Unnamed dream-moon
Elemental Affinity: Moon, Psychic, Aurora
Magical Prowess: ★★★★☆
Technology Level: Soul- and dream-based constructs, bio-crystalline architecture

1.1 Physical Appearance

  • Slender humanoids with opalescent, softly glowing skin.
  • Long hair that drifts like nebula dust.
  • Lower body resembles a smooth, glistening slug-tail.
  • Large luminous “dream eyes.”
  • During metamorphosis they manifest temporary wings of light / aurora plumes.

1.2 Core Traits

  • Born as larvae → chrysalis → adult dream-shapers.
  • Walk dreams, harvest emotional energy, and condense it into Dream Pearls.
  • Strong telepathy and empathy; can weave auroral light and mana currents.
  • Avoid direct violence; excel in psychic defense and emotional harmonics.

2. Yasin

Planet of Origin: Haven-Serra
Elemental Affinity: Light & Shadow (by sect)
Magical Prowess: ★★☆☆☆–★★★★☆ (varies)
Technology Level: Tribal mystic-tech, divine relic forging

2.1 Shared Anatomy

  • Serpentine lower body with long scaled tail.
  • Upright torso with humanoid arms and clawed hands.
  • Cobra-like hood framed by feather-spines.
  • Narrow, predatory face; slit pupils; venomous fangs.

2.2 White Cobra Sect

Role: Healers, historians, spiritual mediators

Visual Cues

  • White, silver, and pale-gold feathers and scales.
  • Soft luminous hood markings.
  • Calm posture, open hands, ceremonial jewelry and robes.

Key Traits

  • Healing venom; purifying breath and sigil-magic.
  • Trance-songs that soothe beasts and cleanse curses.
  • Operate shrines and sanctuaries; serve as judges and diplomats.

2.3 Black Asp Sect

Role: Executioners, elite soldiers, shadow-hunters

Visual Cues

  • Dark scales, black hoods, green/violet feather-spines.
  • Lean, aggressive build; segmented armor and blade-like feathers.
  • Shorter feathered crest used to show rank (longer = higher rank).

Key Traits

  • Shadow-infused venom and stealth tactics.
  • Specialized in hunting curses, monsters, and traitors.
  • Highly stratified ranking system, unlike the egalitarian White Cobra Sect.

3. Crole

Planet of Origin: Gulik (frozen world)
Elemental Affinity: Ice
Magical Prowess: ★☆☆☆☆
Technology Level: Stone-age tools amplified by rare crystal weapons

3.1 Species Overview

The Crole are a half-human, half-insect people who survived near extinction in endless blizzards. Their population rebuilt itself inside underground sanctuaries free from the worst of the ice storms, eventually expanding into multiple fiercely competing tribes.

3.2 Dual Forms

Insect Form

  • Fully cold-immune, powerful and agile.
  • Sterile: cannot reproduce in this state.
  • Used for hunting, war, and survival on the surface.

Humanoid Form

  • Human-like with residual insect traits.
  • Vulnerable to the cold, cannot survive long outside.
  • Fertile: used only for reproduction; revealing this form is an act of extreme trust and vulnerability.

Most Crole choose to remain in insect form permanently; many never learn what their true humanoid face looks like.

3.3 Male Crole

  • Mantis-centaur origin concept refined to bipedal mantis-humanoid.
  • Digitigrade legs with clawed feet.
  • Large raptorial forearms or scythe-like blades.
  • Hard chitin plating over chest, thighs, and shoulders.
  • Tribal paint made from fallen beasts to mark tribe, rank, and achievements.

3.4 Female Crole

  • Based on the poodle moth: thick insulating fur and large patterned wings.
  • Slender torso with long legs and taloned feet.
  • Soft chitin at joints, dense fluff around neck and hips.

Variants:

  • Weavers – shave portions of fur to provide insulation in nurseries and tunnels.
  • Brood Mothers / Caretakers – cuter, rounder forms with signals of high fertility.
  • Monstrous Warriors – bulkier, more bestial silhouettes for elite defense.
  • Standard Slender Form – the most common morphology.

4. Midomi

Planet of Origin: Sofado
Elemental Affinity: Lunar & Devil
Magical Prowess: ★★☆☆☆
Technology Level: Roughly human-equivalent, adapted to multi-limb ergonomics

4.1 Core Species Traits

  • All-male humanoid species.
  • White skin, brown hair, with seven prehensile tentacles emerging from the back.
  • Two base color morphs:
    • Pink Morph – slim, boyish build; bright pink tentacles.
    • Green Morph – tall, muscular build; vivid green tentacles.
  • Their reproduction uses one specialized tentacle as a gamete-producing organ (non-anatomical / non-explicit design).

4.2 Pink Morph — Base Form

Height: ~6'3"
Build: Slender, adolescent-boy aesthetic

Role:

  • Emotional and cultural heart of Midomi society.
  • Primary impregnators of Green Morph egg carriers.
  • Frequently involved in arts, spirituality, and interpersonal networks.

4.3 Green Morph — Base Form

Height: 7'5"–8'0"
Build: Heavily muscular

Role:

  • Warriors, hunters, tacticians, and leadership candidates.
  • Often perceived as intimidating due to size and temperament.
  • Tend toward darker emotional expression but possess high intelligence.

5. Pink Midomi High-Breed Variants

5.1 Matriarch

  • Some Pink Morphs transform into a gigantic tentacular entity with a core mind.
  • Creates a psychic hive-mind of surrounding eggs known as the Dream Network.
  • Eggs placed within the Matriarch’s radius enter the Network and share emotions, memories, and dreams.
  • Worshipped by a devoted faction as a living god.

5.2 Pastor

  • Pink High Breed with an alien-like face and trailing tentacles from head and back.
  • Serves as planetary peacekeeper and diplomat.
  • Specializes in telepathic negotiation, conflict de-escalation, and interstellar relations.

5.3 Custodian

  • Appears as a large pink tentacle supporting a featureless human head.
  • Silent enforcers and covert security.
  • Can become invisible and unleash Brain Shock – a focused psychic blast:
    • One blast = severe disorientation.
    • Three successive blasts = total mental collapse.

5.4 Archangel

  • All-white body, black mask-like face, bright red eyes.
  • Elite bodyguards dedicated to nurseries, hospitals, and all reproductive facilities.
  • Combine physical strength with precise psychic shielding; rarely leave their wards.

5.5 Ethereal Breed

  • Can pass for fully human at a glance.
  • Infertile, but bear a cosmic birthmark on their back that can manifest ghostly ethereal tentacles.
  • Specialists in long-range telepathy, information gathering, and espionage.

5.6 Nursemaid

  • Elongated head, body composed almost entirely of tentacles, classic alien eyes.
  • Rare Pink High Breed with extremely strong psychic powers.
  • Assigned to protect and attend the Midomi Prime Minister.
  • Function as a “shadow security team,” rarely seen by the public.

6. Green Midomi High-Breed Variants

All Green High Breeds are tied to the Midomi military or defense structure and emphasize brute force supported by high intelligence.

6.1 Tormentor

  • All-green head; white body; tentacles packed with toxins.
  • Right tentacles: lethal hallucinogenic poison.
  • Left tentacles: neurotoxin that paralyzes legs and kills over two weeks.
  • Used for private executions and interrogations.

6.2 Champion

  • Smooth shell-like helmet with two small antennae.
  • Idealized warrior physique; apex soldiers and hunters.
  • Exceptional night vision and tracking skills.
  • Permitted four Midomi wives; special nurseries focus on breeding more Champions.

6.3 Tactician

  • Base form appears as a muscular human with minimal tentacles visible.
  • Ascended form: heavy tentacles, green skin, monstrous visage.
  • Brilliant strategists and military advisors.
  • Limited psychic ability: weak telekinesis for battlefield control.

6.4 Warlord

  • Massive, heavy tentacle mass projecting from back and shoulders.
  • Can lift and tear apart war machines and heavy weaponry.
  • Born with weak hearts that may rupture under extreme adrenaline.
  • Deployed only to turn the tide of critical battles.

6.5 Olympian

  • White-bodied High Breed with large webbed feet.
  • Serve as honor guard of the Midomi homeworld and as elite police.
  • While Archangels guard medical and reproductive sites, Olympians guard everything else.

6.6 Titan

  • Huge, bestial High Breed driven by raw rage rather than intellect.
  • Imprints on an infertile Pink Morph handler at hatching.
  • Absolutely loyal to their handler but dangerously unstable.
  • Entire society focuses on keeping Titans calm and protected due to their destructive potential.

6.7 Atlas

  • White body, heavily reinforced tentacles capable of blocking gunfire and artillery.
  • Mask-like tentacle structures around the eyes for additional protection.
  • Hyper-intelligent and often serve as Prime Ministers or top-level leaders.
  • Embody the ideal of Midomi leadership: power, restraint, and strategy.

6.8 “Hunk” Variant

  • Genetically ordinary Green Morphs with particularly striking features.
  • Physically identical in ability to standard Green Morphs.
  • Considered exotic and extremely attractive by Pink Morphs.
  • Socially treated as desirable partners despite small population.
