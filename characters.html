Character Hall of Fame

A small selection of recurring and flagship characters. These are some of the hearts that carry their worlds – the ones I revisit, rewrite, and watch grow across arcs and timelines.

Jay Stellazio & the Three Suitors

Jay Stellazio

A soft-spoken summoner with the Double Star Element, Jay is the emotional center of his sprawling family. He balances duty, fear, and tenderness while navigating dangerous suitors and the expectations placed on his power.

Stellazio Family Saga

Lilith

A Moon-element Shadow Reaver and fiercely protective sister, Lilith is both executioner and guardian. Her love is brutal, uncompromising, and often terrifying – especially toward anyone who harms her siblings.

Stellazio Family Saga

Xane

A Dark Paladin scarred by war and mercenary work, Xane is quiet and gentle at home, monstrous in battle. He struggles with trauma, but finds grounding in the small acts of care he offers his family.

Celestine & the Runaway Angels

Celestine

A runaway angel stitched together by survival, Celestine lives between divinity and humanity. Their story is one of escape, chosen family, and the complicated politics of Heaven.

Corbin’s Confectionary

Corbin

A gentle soul dropped into a magical bakery, Corbin learns to find stability and joy through routine, sweetness, and the people who choose to keep him around.

Corbin’s Confectionary

Luca

Warm, clingy, and quietly devoted, Luca wraps himself around Corbin like a living security blanket. He’s proof that comfort can be just as magical as any spell.
